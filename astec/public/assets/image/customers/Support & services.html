How we work?

Our project lifecycle is an iterative process. We split your project into stages in order to deliver results quickly and make your budget planning easy. For each stage of the project we go trough the process below:

FREE

CONSULTATION

Before we even start, we will analyze your business requirements in depth, and offer some advice on technology choice & project roadmap. For free.

GET IT NOW

1

PRICE & TIME ESTIMATE

For every new stage of development, we will prepare a price quotation along with an estimated time of arrival (ETA).

2

DESIGN

In this phase we will arrive at the final prototype for your idea. We will provide you with visual concepts (mockups) and adjust them based on your feedback.

3

DEVELOPMENT

After we make sure that the visual side of your idea is intuitive & gorgeous, it is time to make it functional. We write clean code in accordance best practices in the industry.

4

REVIEW & TESTING

We test developed functionalities thoroughly and provide you with a Timesheet, with detailed descriptions of completed tasks.
